ABOUT

In Time Tec helps clients understand what business needs are not being met within the software development lifecycle and what bottlenecks are causing efficiency issues. Our primary role is to free up an organization’s valuable resources so it is better able to capitalize on its own strengths and provide a quality and satisfactory product for the end customer.

Web Development

If an application runs in a browser, we can help with development, testing, and deployment.

Desktop Development

Common desktop development needs involve high-security applications that require hosting within an organization’s firewall, or legacy applications that exist within an environment and cannot be eliminated.

Embedded Development

Embedded development is the building of software that runs very particular machines (e.g., printers or televisions). Embedded software comes with a host of problems that In Time Tec has experience working through. In short, we help your teams deliver on time within your budget constraints, and provided immediate value across your development lifecycle. Contact us today, and together we can explore how to solve your development service needs.





OUR COMMITMENT

Our commitment is to enable customers to achieve business objectives faster, with less cost. According to industry expert, Geoffrey Moore, every company has both core and context work. Core work is what makes you different. Context work is non-core work that needs to get done. In Time Tec takes on you context work, allowing you to keep your brightest and most valuable talent focused on innovation.
